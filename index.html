Bastions on AWS

Sometimes your services and platforms on AWS still require you to have a network, even when you’re serverless. For the platform at work my team is responsible for this is the boat we continually sail in, taking advantage of AWS Fargate, S3, ElastiCache, but not AWS Bastion Service. Why? Because it doesn’t exist yet—fully at least. Sometimes, you just need to get on a local network and play with things.
